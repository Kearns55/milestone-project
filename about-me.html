About Me

Fáilte!

I am Wearns welcome to my portfolio and I hope you like what you see! I am a multi-medium artist working mostly with acylic on canvas but have been known to do digital art designing stickers and tattoos. My work is deeply-rooted in street-culture with a touch of Irish influence. I combine bold colors, traditional tattoo styles, religious figures and Irish culture in my art. Born and raised in Dublin, Ireland but currently based in Stockholm, Sweden. The Irish community in stockholm is something i was pleasently surprised to find and was welcomed in with open arms as my work was displayed in Arrivals Artists exhibiton just this summer (as pictured). Creating art was always just a hobby for me but the more i make the more i fall in love with the craft. My commissions are currently open so please dont hesitate to get in touch either by Instagram or my commission request form you can find on this site. Take it easy and mind yourself, Sláinte!
-Wearns
